FAQs

See our frequently asked questions here

Who is eligible to open an account with Meridian Banking?

To open an account with us, you must be at least 18 years of age, a UK resident, and hold a valid form of government-issued identification such as a passport or driving licence.

Can I hold multiple accounts with Meridian Banking?

Yes. Eligible customers may hold a current account, savings account, and additional specialist accounts simultaneously.

What types of mortgages does Meridian Banking offer?

We offer fixed-rate, variable-rate, and tracker mortgages. You can use our Mortgage Calculator to get an estimate of your monthly repayments before applying.

What factors affect my mortgage eligibility?

Eligibility is assessed based on your credit history, income, existing financial commitments, and the size of your deposit.

What should I do if I suspect fraudulent activity on my account?

Contact our fraud team immediately on our dedicated helpline. We recommend you do not share any account details and change your password as a precaution.

Are there any monthly fees for holding a current account?

Our standard current account carries no monthly fee. Premium account tiers may carry a monthly charge in exchange for additional benefits.

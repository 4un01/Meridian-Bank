Information About Our Overdrafts

Meridian Bank helps you stay protected when life throws unexpected expenses your way with flexible overdraft solutions designed to keep you moving forward. Our Standard Overdraft Coverage automatically covers debit card purchases, checks, and ACH payments when your balance runs short — subject to eligibility — so essential transactions go through without interruption. Prefer more control? Opt into Overdraft Protection Link, which automatically transfers funds from your linked Meridian savings account or line of credit to cover shortfalls, typically at a lower cost than standard overdraft fees. For eligible customers, our Overdraft Line of Credit provides a dedicated safety net with competitive interest rates — borrow only what you need, repay on your schedule, and avoid declined transactions entirely. We believe in transparency, so you'll always know where you stand with real-time balance alerts, easy-to-understand fee disclosures, and a daily overdraft fee cap that limits costs during tough weeks. Plus, our Grace Period gives you until midnight the next business day to bring your account positive and avoid fees altogether. At Meridian, we don't just cover overdrafts — we help you manage them wisely with financial wellness tools, spending insights, and personalized guidance to reduce reliance over time.
